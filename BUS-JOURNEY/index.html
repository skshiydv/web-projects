Welcome to Maarg - Your Path to Seamless Public Transport

Are you tired of juggling multiple cards and apps for public transport, wishing for a simpler and more efficient solution? Look no further! Maarg is here to revolutionize your journey with a one-tap solution that brings together all your mobility needs.

Why choose Maarg over conventional ticketing?

  • All-in-One Super Card: Maarg introduces the concept of the "Super Card." This is not just a card; it's your key to an effortless commute. Whether you're traveling by train, bus, or e-rickshaw, this card has you covered. No more hassle of managing multiple cards or apps.

  • Best User Experience: We take pride in offering you the best user experience. Our web application is designed with your convenience in mind. It's easy to use and visually appealing, making your journey smoother and more enjoyable.

    • How It Works:

    1. Apply for Your Super Card: Getting started is a breeze. Apply for your Super Card, and it will be your gateway to a hassle-free commute.
    2. Login to our Web Interface: Access our user-friendly web interface to manage your card, check balances, and plan your journeys.
    3. Recharge Your Card: Top up your Super Card with a few clicks. No more standing in long queues or searching for recharge stations. Maarg puts the power in your hands.
    4. Secure Your Card: In case your Super Card is lost or stolen, don't worry. You can instantly block it to prevent any unauthorized usage.

    Extra Perks:

    1. Family-Friendly: Maarg understands the importance of family. You can easily add family members to a single card, ensuring a convenient and cost-effective way to travel together.
    2. Real-Time Updates: Maarg isn't just about cards. We also provide real-time information about bus locations, occupancy, routes, deducted fare, last transactions and journeys and even nearby restaurants and places. You'll always be in the know.

    ABOUT

    Welcome to the Public Transport Revolution project, your one-stop solution for revolutionizing Indian public transport using web and in-bus hardware. This project aims to provide a seamless user experience for commuters, ready to support all means of transport. Offering features like -- Checking and recharging card balance Accessing the record of past journeys Adding family members to existing cards with cryptographic unique IDs Providing feedback, tracking bus live locations, and exploring routes.

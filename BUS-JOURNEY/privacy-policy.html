Privacy Policy

At Maarg, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register for our services, we collect your name and phone number to create your profile.

  • Card Parameters: To provide you with our "Super Card" services, we collect card-specific details, including its name and unique identifier (UID).

  • Location Data: We track the real-time location of our buses using specialized onboard computers. Your device's location is not used for this purpose.

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: We use your data to personalize your experience, show nearby places and bus information, and facilitate easy card management.

  • Service Improvement: Data is used to improve our services, including enhancing user experience and analyzing patterns for service enhancements.

Information Sharing

  • Third-Party Payment Services: We may share data with third-party services for analytics, marketing, and payment processing, but your information is always protected using SHA-256 Encryption

Data Security

  • We employ advanced security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely

Access and Control

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage your card and profile details through our web interface

Updates to Privacy Policy

  • We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this policy regularly for any changes.
